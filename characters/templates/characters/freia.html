FREIA NOCTIS

THE HEIR OF SILENCE - CHRONICLE OF PAIN AND POWER

PROLOGUE: DYNASTY IN THE SHADOW OF SUPREME PRINCIPLES

Freia Noctis — the only surviving daughter and heir of King Rein and Queen Amilla, rulers of Imperium Noctis, a civilization that elevated the law of power to the absolute. But behind their cruelty lies an abyss of personal tragedy: they were hostages to the Supreme Principles — metaphysical forces that prescribed them to ensure reality's survival at the cost of their daughter's soul. They wore masks of heartless tyrants to hide their despair.

CHILDHOOD: ENGINEERING OF HELL (AGE 0-9)

Her upbringing was not training, but surgical removal of humanity, conducted with the silent consent of the Principles.

0-3 Years Strict, militarized care. Her first "toys" were replicas of torture instruments.
3-6 Years Beginning of active "tempering". Observation of executions, physical and chemical torture under the guidance of The Alchemist.
6-9 Years Practice. Personal conduct of executions, deadly tournaments, labyrinths with starving beasts under the supervision of equally unfree servants.

THE ONLY LIGHT THEY ALLOWED THEMSELVES TO GIVE

During this time, she gained her only friend — Rudzi, a teenage butler from Imperium Lumen. He secretly showed her kindness, becoming living proof that compassion exists. Her parents saw this, and their souls were torn by internal struggle. They saw her smile for the first time, and part of them yearned to leave everything as it was. But the dictate of the Principles and the memory of their eldest daughter, Bellona, who died due to "weakness", were relentless. Rudzi disappeared — not killed, but exiled through a portal, becoming an eternal, unhealing wound in her heart.

EIDOLON EVOLUTION: BIRTH OF THE WEAPON

At age 10, in the final tournament, on the brink of death from the strongest opponent, her veins ignited with purple radiance, and her bones restructured in sync with agony. She acquired Eidolon Evolution — power purchased at the price of all her previous pain, and killed her opponent. For her parents, this was a bitter triumph — their method "worked".

WAR: TEMPERING OF STEEL (AGE 10-15)

She was sent to the front of the Five-Year War. She became an instrument of genocide, unaware that in one of the raids, her hand killed Rudzi's mother. Prince Lumen III, who loved her and believed in peace, fell by her blade during his confession. Each killing was for her parents another nail in the coffin of their own humanity, but they saw in it the final, harshest lesson of survival.

PERSONALITY: SHADOWS CAST BY FLAME

The result was a broken psyche:

Cynicism: Kindness and love are illusions of the weak.
Aromantic: Incapable of romantic attachment.
Cognitive Dissonance: Hates parents but grateful to them for strength.
Only Vulnerability: Cats — the last bastion of trampled humanity.

POWERS AND ABILITIES

Her power — not a gift, but scars turned into weapons:

Evocatio Ensis

Type: Innate eidolon-weapon
Weight: Infinite for others, 0 for Freia
Special: Ignores conceptual defenses, instant summon/dismiss

Decem Milia Ictuum

Effect: 10,000 attacks in 5 seconds
Range: 360° radius
Use: Breaking mass defenses, group elimination

Clausura Ensium

Type: Ultimate ability
Effect: Teleports target to isolated space, light-speed blade attacks for 10 seconds
Cost: 80% energy reserve, 2 weeks recovery

"SILENTIUM" — Her battle cry and key to the soul. Activated only in absolute solitude within 5 meters, creating a zone where sound is silenced and reality's processes halt. Any external gaze destroys it, making Silentium the perfect sensory perimeter — a metaphor for her soul: she is strongest when no one sees her.

HEIR OF DARKNESS: QUEEN OF THE SILENT KINGDOM

When the cup of suffering overflows, and pain threatens to dissolve the last boundaries of consciousness, the Heir of Darkness is born — the absolute monarch of pain, imprisoned in the darkest crypts of Freia's soul. This is the Unrested soul of Freia's dead sister named Bellona, who was killed by marauders and should have been the future ruler. Her sister became her vessel from birth.

Transformation Trigger: Mortal threat breaking natural combat flow ("cheating" abilities) or Freia's existential crisis
Visual Manifestation: Irises absorb all light, becoming absolutely black, while pupils burn poisonous yellow and blood red
Siphō Aeterna

Effect: Permanently blocks "cheating" abilities that break battle rules
Against Cheaters: Burns out the very possibility of using the ability at soul/concept level
Limitation: Doesn't work on true monarchs, princes of blood, or S-class beings whose nature is world's foundation

THE ULTIMATE PRICE

Each activation of the Heir of Darkness is a step toward oblivion. After the fifth manifestation, Freia's personality will be irreversibly absorbed, and her body will become an eternal vessel for this cold, arrogant queen. Her mind, her "palace," will fall, and only a perfect weapon will remain in the world, devoid of the last traces of that girl who once loved kittens.

STATUS: FUGITIVE FROM THE PAST, SEEKER OF PEACE

Freia — a princess in flight, a recluse anti-hero. Her goal is not to save worlds, but to find absolute peace, torn between escaping her parents' legacy and searching for the last grains of humanity in memories of Rudzi and the silent affection of cats.
